## Marketing Campaign Analysis

### Overview
Analyzed a bank marketing campaign dataset to understand factors influencing customer subscription to term deposits and to build predictive models for campaign effectiveness.

### Dataset
- Source: Bank marketing campaign data
- Records: Customer demographics, financial attributes, and campaign interaction details
- Target variable: Term deposit subscription (yes/no)

### Techniques Used
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory data analysis (EDA)
- Feature encoding and scaling
- Classification modeling and evaluation

### Business Value
Helps marketing teams improve targeting strategies, reduce campaign costs, and increase conversion rates.
